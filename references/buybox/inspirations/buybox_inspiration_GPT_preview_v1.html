4. Message Amazon — version EN copiable

Title

Retail Availability / Inventory Reliability issue – ASIN B00PVPXVBE had dormant inventory but Featured Offer views dropped to zero

Message

Hello,

We need Amazon’s support to investigate ASIN B00PVPXVBE.

This ASIN is a high-performing item, but Amazon Retail appears to have stopped exposing the offer despite having dormant inventory.

Observed facts:
- ASIN: B00PVPXVBE
- Product: COGEX 80017 padlock 30 mm
- Amazon previously showed approximately 645 units of dormant inventory.
- Retail sales stopped while Amazon still appeared to have inventory.
- Featured Offer page views dropped to 0 in May 2026.
- Current Amazon sellable inventory now appears to be only 5 units.
- The current Featured Offer / Buy Box is held by a 3P seller, not Amazon Retail.
- The ASIN has a history of PO / delivery issues, including PO non-acceptance, delivery defects and BOL / ASN mismatch events.

This suggests the issue was not initially caused by a lack of physical inventory, but may be linked to inventory reliability, receiving reconciliation, Retail Availability suppression, or stock reclassification.

Could you please confirm:
1. Why Amazon Retail stopped exposing the offer while dormant inventory was still available?
2. Whether the previous 645 units were sellable, reserved, in transfer, unsellable, under investigation, or otherwise unavailable to the Retail offer?
3. Why the stock was recently adjusted from approximately 645 units to only 5 sellable units?
4. Whether any receiving / inventory reconciliation or stock correction occurred?
5. Whether the ASIN is impacted by Retail Availability suppression, inventory management suppression, purchase hold, do-not-reorder, or instock restriction?
6. Whether prior ASN / BOL / receiving reconciliation issues contributed to the inventory being treated as unreliable or non-buyable?
7. What corrective action is required to restore Amazon Retail availability and Featured Offer eligibility?

We are implementing corrective actions on the inbound process to prevent ASN / BOL / appointment mismatches and would like Amazon to review whether this ASIN can be re-enabled for Amazon Retail Featured Offer once the inventory reliability issue is resolved.
